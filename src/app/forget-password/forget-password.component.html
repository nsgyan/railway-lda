Land Acquisition Programme under District Administration

Land Acquisition - Beneficiary & Budget Management System (BBMS) is a web-based online software application. BBMS is being rolled out with the objective of tracking the beneficiaries and funds released to beneficiaries under the acquisition of the land and ensuring real-time reporting.

BBMS has established an interface with the ICICI CIB Banking System (CIB) to provide the one window dashboard for the Department.
